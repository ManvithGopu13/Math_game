How to play

Answer each equation before the bar empties. Each correct answer refills time slightly and increases difficulty. Keep a streak to raise your score multiplier and approach ∞.

  • Space starts/pauses, Enter submits.
  • Difficulty scales: bigger numbers, more operations, and powers if enabled.
  • High score is saved locally.
